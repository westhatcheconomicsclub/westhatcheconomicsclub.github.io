About Our Club

Our West Hatch Economics Club is a platform for students passionate about economics, finance, and global markets. We organize discussions, workshops, and events to foster learning and networking opportunities.

Our Mission

To provide a collaborative environment where students can explore economic concepts beyond the classroom, develop critical thinking skills, and prepare for university and careers in economics and related fields.

Our Vision

To become a leading platform for economics education and discussion at West Hatch, fostering a community of informed and engaged young economists.

What We Do

Discussion Sessions

Regular meetings to discuss current economic issues and topics of interest.

Competitions

Participate in local and national economics competitions as a team.

Study Groups

Collaborative study sessions to support academic achievement in economics.

Join Us

The West Hatch Economics Club welcomes students of all levels who are interested in economics. Whether you're currently studying economics or just curious about the subject, we'd love to have you join us!
